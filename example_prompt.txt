A young witch named Luna discovers she has the power to communicate with spirits.
On her 16th birthday, a mysterious ghost appears and tells her she must save the spirit world from an ancient curse.
Luna embarks on a magical adventure through haunted forests and mystical temples.
She meets friendly spirits who help her along the way, but also faces dark creatures trying to stop her.
The story should be 3-4 pages with an exciting climax and hopeful ending.
